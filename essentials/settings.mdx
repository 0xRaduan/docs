On the settings page, we have several pages that help you setup and manage your account.

## Account Settings

On this page you can change your name, create/see your [AI Inbox](/essentials/ai-inbox), and see your current weekly limits for summaries.

[Link to the Account Settings page](https://summate.io/settings/account)
