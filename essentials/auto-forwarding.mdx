## Overview

Instead of subscribing to all newsletters manually again, you can set up auto-forwarding and send a copy of selected newsletters from your main inbox.

The overall process typically takes about 5 minutes of time, and generally requires to:

- add your `@inbox.summate.io` email to your approved forwarding addresses
- setup filters to forward newsletters to your `@inbox.summate.io` email
